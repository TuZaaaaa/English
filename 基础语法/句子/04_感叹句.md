# 感叹句


## 词性对比
- What
  - 作 n.
    - eg: What do you what? => You want what.
  - 作 adj.
    - eg: What color do you what? => You want what color.
- How
  - 作 adv.
    - eg: How do you come here? => You how come here.


## What 结构公式

- What + [名词短语] + (主+谓)
  - eg: What +  a good body + he is

## How 结构公式

- How + v./adj./adv.
  - eg: How good (the boy is)!
