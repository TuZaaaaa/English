# 同位语
> 对另一个**名词**解释说明的**名词**
> 类似 1:1 的关系

## 同位语 vs 宾补 vs 定语

- 同位语
  - 词性：
    - 名词 eg: Tom, **a high school student**, is a nice guy.
  - 顺序：可变/可不变
  - 范围：不变 1:1
- 宾补
  - 词性：
    - 名词 eg: She made me **a fool**.
    - 形容词 eg: I found the book **interesting**.
    - 副词 eg: I found her **out**.
    - 介词 eg: I found her in the **classroom**.
    - 不定式 eg: I asked her **to come**.
    - 现在分词 eg: I found her **reading a book**.
    - 过去分词
      - eg: I found her **gone**. vi.
      - eg: I found my bike **fixed**. vt.
  - 顺序：不变
  - 范围：不变
- 定语
  - 词性：
    - 副词 eg: People (living) **here** are very nice.
    - 形容词 eg: The man **next to me** is Tom.
    - 介词 eg: The boy **under the tree** is Tom.
    - 不定式 eg: I have something **to tell you**.
    - 现在分词 eg: The girl **sitting over there** is Summer.
    - 过去分词 eg: The house **built last year** has been sold out.
  - 顺序：变序
  - 范围：变小


## 同位语 vs 宾补

- eg:
  - 同位语：I love him **Morty**. 1:1
  - 宾补：His father named him **Morty**. 部分（名字）

## 宾补 vs 后置定语

- eg:
  - 宾补：I saw the boy **playing basketball**. 没有限定 the boy 的范围
  - 后置定语：The boy **playing basketball** is Tom. 限定了 the boy 的范围

## 同位语 vs 后置定语

- eg:
  - 同位语：Tom, who is a student, studies hard.
  - 后置定语：Tom, a student, studies hard.
